<objective>
Prevent Ctrl+Cmd+F (macOS native fullscreen shortcut) from being captured by the app's Cmd+F search handler in CommitGraph.svelte.

Purpose: The Cmd+F handler in CommitGraph.svelte uses capture-phase event listening and checks `(e.metaKey || e.ctrlKey) && e.key === 'f'`. When the user presses Ctrl+Cmd+F, both `metaKey` and `ctrlKey` are true, so the handler intercepts the event and opens the search bar instead of letting the OS toggle fullscreen.

Output: Updated keydown guard that passes through Ctrl+Cmd+F to the system.
</objective>
